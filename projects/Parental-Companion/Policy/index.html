Parent Companion ("we", "us", or "our") provides a baby routine tracking app. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

Information We Collect

Account data: if you create an account, we may collect your email address and basic profile details.

App data you enter: baby routines, notes, schedules, growth stats, and caretaker sharing details.

Device data: device identifiers, OS version, and app performance data for diagnostics.

How We Use Information

We use your information to provide core app functionality, sync data across devices, improve features, and keep the app secure.

Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with service providers (such as cloud hosting or analytics) that help us operate the app, under contracts that limit their use.

Data Retention

We keep your data while your account is active. You may request deletion at any time.

Security

We apply reasonable safeguards to protect your data, but no system is 100% secure.

Children's Privacy

The app is intended for parents or guardians. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Your Choices

You can update or delete your data in the app. To request account deletion, contact us.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. We will post updates on this page and revise the effective date.
